Spherical Columella Beads

Spherical beads from the Fredricks site occurred with barrel/cylinders, runtees, and wampum around the neck and chest area of Burial 2. All of these spherical beads were very badly preserved. It appeared from the few intact surfaces that they were of a more finished quality (i.e., smoother and more polished) than those from the earlier Wall site. Measurements ranged from 4.6-7.2 mm in length and from 5.4-7.3 mm in diameter.
