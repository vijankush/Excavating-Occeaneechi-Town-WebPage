Metal Resource Group

Artifacts placed in this group include nine pieces of wire, 23 pieces of metal scrap (lead and iron), 98 pieces of cut sheet-metal strips (mostly of brass), and 11 wire fragments. Many of these pieces probably represent by-products of aboriginally modified Euroamerican artifacts.
